Settings

Company info
To change your registered business details, contact Monalisa Tech Solutions support.
Stock aging policy — field staff
Applies to Agents, Team Leaders, and Managers. Sets when a device's color changes and when it's pulled into Recovery.
Anything older than the warning cutoff turns red and moves into Recovery automatically.
Stock aging policy — shop owners
Set separately from field staff, since shop stock often moves differently.
Commission per device sold
A fixed UGX amount, paid directly to whoever made the sale
A Manager or Team Leader who personally makes a sale earns this same direct amount too — they're not excluded from selling.
Cumulative commission
Paid automatically the moment someone in their team sells — no extra step
This is separate from direct commission above. A Team Leader earns this every time ANY agent under them sells a device — automatically, instantly, on top of anything they earn from their own personal sales.
