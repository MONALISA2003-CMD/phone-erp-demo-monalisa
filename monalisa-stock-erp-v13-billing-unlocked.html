Stock Allocation

Who can allocate to whom
CEO & Administrators
Can allocate stock to Managers, Team Leaders, or Shop Owners directly from CEO master stock
Managers & Team Leaders
Can only allocate stock they've already received, and only down to Agents under them
Agents & Shop Owners
Receive stock only. They sell it or return it — they cannot allocate onward to anyone

2

4
Manager/TL sends to Agent
5
Agent confirms receipt

Agent sells or returns
