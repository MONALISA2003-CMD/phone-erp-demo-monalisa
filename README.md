MONALISA STOCK ERP

Built for People Who Move Stock, Close Sales, and Grow Businesses

A practical inventory, sales, financing, and recovery management platform designed for mobile phone distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and field sales teams.

---

About the Project

MONALISA STOCK ERP was created to address everyday operational challenges faced by growing phone businesses.

As inventory moves between warehouses, managers, team leaders, agents, finance partners, and customers, maintaining visibility becomes increasingly difficult. Businesses often struggle with tracking devices, monitoring sales performance, managing financed inventory, and generating reliable reports.

This platform brings those activities together into a single environment, making it easier to understand what is happening across the business and where attention is needed.

The focus is simple:

- Improve visibility
- Strengthen accountability
- Simplify reporting
- Support better decision-making

---

Why This Project Exists

Many businesses still rely on spreadsheets, manual reports, phone calls, and disconnected systems to manage inventory and sales operations.

As operations expand, this often leads to:

- Missing or misplaced inventory
- Delayed reporting
- Limited operational visibility
- Difficult recovery processes
- Inconsistent performance tracking
- Reduced accountability

MONALISA STOCK ERP was designed to provide a structured and reliable way to manage these processes while remaining easy to use.

Technology Stack

This demonstration was intentionally built using:

- HTML5
- CSS3
- Vanilla JavaScript

No external frontend frameworks were used.

---

Why HTML5?

HTML provides the foundation of the platform.

Benefits include:

- Broad browser compatibility
- Reliable structure
- Long-term maintainability
- Lightweight deployment

---

Why CSS3?

CSS controls the visual presentation and responsive layout.

Benefits include:

- Responsive design
- Mobile optimization
- Consistent user experience
- Improved readability

The interface adapts naturally across phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

---

Why Vanilla JavaScript?

The demonstration uses pure JavaScript rather than external frameworks.

This approach was chosen because it provides:

Lightweight Performance

The application loads quickly and remains responsive because there are no large framework dependencies.

Simpler Maintenance

Developers can understand and modify the project without framework-specific knowledge.

Greater Flexibility

Every interaction is built directly, providing complete control over functionality and user experience.

Long-Term Stability

The project avoids dependency conflicts and framework-related upgrade issues.

Design Philosophy

Good software should support the people using it.

The platform was designed around practical workflows rather than unnecessary complexity.

Key principles include:

- Clear navigation
- Consistent layouts
- Readable information
- Minimal learning curve
- Efficient workflows

The objective is to help users focus on running the business rather than learning software.

---

Mobile-First Experience

Business activities rarely happen from a single location.

Managers, agents, and business owners frequently rely on mobile devices while travelling, supervising teams, or meeting customers.

For this reason, responsiveness was considered from the beginning.

The platform works across:

- Smartphones
- Tablets
- Laptops
- Desktop Computers

Without sacrificing usability or readability.
